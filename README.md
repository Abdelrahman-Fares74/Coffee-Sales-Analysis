Coffee Sales Dashboard:

This project presents an interactive Coffee Sales Dashboard designed to analyze sales performance and provide business insights. The dashboard visualizes key metrics such as total sales, number of transactions, and average price, helping users quickly understand overall performance.

It includes multiple visualizations to explore sales patterns, including:

Sales Trend by Month: Displays monthly changes in sales amount and transactions.

Sales by Time of Day: Shows how sales are distributed between morning, afternoon, and night.

Sales by Product: Highlights the best-selling coffee products.

The goal of this dashboard is to transform raw sales data into clear, actionable insights that support better business decisions. It demonstrates skills in data analysis, data visualization, and dashboard design using business intelligence tools.
